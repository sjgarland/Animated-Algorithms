This website was created by Stephen Garland. Much of the material about algorithms on it is based on Dr. Garland's textbook, Introduction to Computer Science with Applications in Pascal, published by Addison-Wesley in 1986. Many of the animations were inspired by Dr. Garland's experiences with Scratch and with Ziro Studio, a web-based platform for physics-based three-dimensional graphics.
